Diversion. Education. Justice.

A UNC student-founded platform to keep Chapel Hill's most vulnerable residents from becoming homeless and educate the UNC community about the underlying causes of mass homelessness. In Chapel Hill, we are the only source of diversion funding - the most immediate and research-backed means to prevent homelessness.
